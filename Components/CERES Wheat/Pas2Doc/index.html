UHumeWheatPartitioning

Module for carbon and nitrogen partitioning on organ level (root, stem, leaves, grains) for wheat see Appendix Diss. Ratjen 2012) C translocation to grains according to experimental results of 103, 94 (see Appendix Diss. Ratjen 2012)

- N concentrations in organs during vegetative growth adapted from TSubPartitioningVegNew (U. Böttcher, Meyer-Schatz), but modified for drought stress according to experimental results of 103 2010 (see Diss. Ratjen Appendix)

- leaf N distribution is calculated for specific leaf layers at anthesis according to experimental results of 103 10 (Appendix Diss. Ratjen )

- N dynamics similar to Bertheloot 2008 (see Appendix Diss. Ratjen )

- sigmoid grain filling (Okt. 2015)

- Partitioning of N deficit according to Ratjen & Kage 2016 (JACS)

- Main authors A.M. Ratjen, U. Böttcher, H. Kage et al.
