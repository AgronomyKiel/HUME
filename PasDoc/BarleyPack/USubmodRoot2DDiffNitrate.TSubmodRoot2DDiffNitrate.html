Fields

Clmin: TPar;

Minimum soil solution concentration [Mol/l], also needed for the numerical solution in the 1D model; note: originally in micromol/l

cl_av: TVar;

Average concentration in the soil solution [Mol/cmˆ3]

c_start: TVar;

Initial concentration [mol/cmˆ3], calculated from the initial N amount; the 1D model can also calculate concentrations

De: TVar;

Effective diffusion coefficient [cmˆ2/s]

Dl: TPar;

Diffusion coefficient of nitrate in free H2O [cmˆ2/s]

minera: TPar;

Mineralization rate [kg N/ha*d]

Mineralization model not yet available but should be implemented for both models

Min_S: TVar;

Mineralization rate [Mol/cmˆ3*s]

N_AmountSoil: TState;

N amount [kg N/ha], also basis for calculating concentrations in the calculation elements; see Kage dissertation p.79 where concentrations of 10.0 micromol/l were assumed

Margins are necessary so that edge effects can be excluded when the root exit points are hexagonally distributed

Cumulative amount of N taken up by the roots [kg N/ha] for the specified depth

Type of uptake calculation
